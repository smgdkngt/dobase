Eight tools, one app.

Install only what you need. Create as many of each as you want — a board per project, a chat per team. Each one can be shared with different collaborators, or kept private. Organize your sidebar with custom groups and drag-and-drop ordering.

Mail

IMAP/SMTP email client with rich text compose, contacts, and threaded conversations. Connect your existing mailbox — and share it with your team.

Board

Kanban boards with drag-and-drop columns, cards, comments, and attachments.

Chat

Real-time messaging with typing indicators, threaded replies, and file sharing.

Docs

Rich text documents with real-time collaborative editing across your team.

Todos

Task lists with due dates, assignments, comments, and file attachments.

Files

File storage with folders, preview, and public link sharing.

Calendar

CalDAV-compatible with recurring events and standalone mode.

Room

Video calls powered by LiveKit, without leaving your workspace.

Your data, your rules.

Dobase runs on your server. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party data sharing. One Rails app, one SQLite database, one Docker container. That is the entire stack.
